Photo Session FAQ

What should I wear?
Wear whatever makes you feel comfortable! Dress for the weather and make sure to coordinate your colors. Remember, years from now your family won't care about your hair or what you are wearing, they just want to see a picture of you!
How long is a photo session?
Each photo session is a different length based on your vision! Sessions can be as short as 30 minutes and last up to 8 hours.
Can I bring my dog or cat?
Yes! I love taking pictures of pets! Advice: Dogs and cats will not always cooperate at a photo session so make sure to bring an extra person.
What is your pricing?
Rates will vary based on location, package and type of session! Contact me for more information.
